Teams

Microsoft Team is an integrated system for connecting your employees in workplace via chat, video meetings and file sharing. It can also be used to integrate app. You can create multiple chat rooms within a team.

The major features that Teams has to offer involve;

  • Direct messages to private users rather than group chats though being in a workspace environment
  • Messages can be replied to using posts, texts, GIFs, custom made memes and images.
  • Allows Instant Messaging services and Voice calls
  • Feature of Video conferencing
  • You can schedule meeting and also share documents.
  • Also allows sharing of material, assignments and quizzes through Office Forms.
Exchange

Exchange online is the Server messaging platform provide by Microsoft that companies can obtain as single serves or via an Office 365 subscription. Exchange helps you integrate on your critical documents and gives you a focused inbox that prioritizes important messages and adapts to your work style, so you can get more done–faster.

Users connected to Exchange can get access to all their mails via Microsoft Outlook with a web browser or with mobile devices using the Outlook mobile app to access emails. Features of Exchange:

  • It is flexible it adapts to your working style and gives you focused inbox so that you can prioritize your important mails to get more done.
  • Allows you to organize your emails in a more structural way which is intelligent, smart and much more interactive.
  • Search option for faster and complete results.
  • You can customize according to your need with the add-ins.
