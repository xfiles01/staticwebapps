Infrastructure

In this age of digitalization it is very important for business to know the importance of network infrastructure and be aware of the challenges and opportunities it presents. Network Infrastructure refers to the sources that connect internet, management, business operations with each other. It comprises of both hardware as well as software. It enables smooth communication between users, services, applications and processes.

Without the right infrastructure in place, you may face poor user experience and other issues that can reduce employee productivity, capital losses and bad impression to your customers.

A typical network infrastructure includes:

Networking Hardware:

  • Routers
  • Switches
  • LAN cards
  • Wireless routers
  • Cables

Networking Software:

  • Network operations and management
  • Operating systems
  • Firewall
  • Network security applications
